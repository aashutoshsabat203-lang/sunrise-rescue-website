About Us

Sunrise Rescue is dedicated to providing food, clothing, and animal rescue services to those in need. Our mission is to bring hope and relief to vulnerable communities and animals alike.

Our Services

Food Distribution

Providing meals to people in need to fight hunger and malnutrition.

Clothing Drives

Collecting and distributing clothes to help people stay warm and comfortable.

Animal Rescue

Rescuing and rehabilitating stray and injured animals to give them a safe life.
